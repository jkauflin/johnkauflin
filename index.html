Welcome!

Welcome to the John J Kauflin personal website.

    Welcome to my website, and ok, it's mostly about me. However, I hope this site also reflects the experiences I've had with all the wonderful friends, family, bandmates, and workmates I've had in my life. It's been my pleasure to know you all. This website is a place where I can collect and present pictures, videos, and music to honor the memory of people and things.

    My Families

    I've been involved with some great and interesting families including my Mom's, my Dad's, my Wife's, and my own. I appreciate my Mom taking all of those snapshots over the years, and my Dad for shooting the 8mm home movies when I was growing up. They are priceless memories of a great childhood.

    My Bands

    Music has been one of the great joys of my life and I've had the honor and privilege of playing with some great musicians, and being part of a wonderful music community
